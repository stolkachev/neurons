InterActor - one who interacts


Commands you can say or type:

"help" or "?" - show this help

"clear" - clear content of the current view

"my name is ..." - introduce yourself

"I am at home" - declare that current location is at home

"reset" - reset current states and return to the default setting

"undo" - restore the last deleted or edited record

"chat", "health", "shopping", "notes" - switch to the corresponding view


Supported languages (type only):

"zh" - Chinese

"en" - English

"fr" - French

"de" - German

"hebrew" - Hebrew

"hindi" - Hindi

"italian" - Italian

"ru" - Russian

"es" - Spanish
